## The Philosophy Behind the Design

**We don't sell. We guide.**

Every email, every page, every touchpoint is designed around one question: *"What does she need to feel right now?"*

- Right after purchase: confidence that she made the right decision
- During the program: momentum, accountability, and connection
- After completion: pride, and a natural next step when she's ready
- If she goes quiet: a caring check-in, not a guilt trip

We never pitch the next product the day after she finishes one. We celebrate first, let it breathe, then bridge to the next step over days — not hours.

## What She Experiences — Program by Program

### 1. Ultimate Hormone Assessment ($47)

**The moment she buys:**
She doesn't get sent to her email inbox. The quiz starts immediately on the confirmation page. No waiting, no friction. She's in the moment — we keep her there.

**After the quiz:**
Her results appear on screen instantly — a full personalized breakdown with what her result means, her top contributing factors, and 3 things she can do this week. Then a beautiful keepsake version lands in her inbox.

**Over the next 10 days:**
- Day 1: Results + report (email backup)
- Day 3: Educational deep-dive specific to her result type (estrogen dominant or balanced)
- Day 6: Honest bridge to the 7-Day Challenge — Dr. P voice, not a sales page
- Day 10: Final touch — one more angle, then done

**What makes it different:** The assessment feels like a real diagnostic experience, not a quiz that ends with "buy my next thing." The value is front-loaded. The upsell comes later, after she's had time to sit with her results.

**Total emails:** 2-6 depending on her behavior

---

### 2. 7-Day Challenge ($47)

**The moment she buys:**
The confirmation page shows her exactly how to access the course — with screenshots of what to look for, step-by-step. If she's new to Kajabi, we walk her through password setup visually right there on the page. Day 1 is already unlocked.

**During the 7 days:**
She gets 4 emails across the week — not 7. Kajabi drips the content daily, so we don't nag her every morning. Instead:
- Day 1: Welcome + "reply and tell me your #1 goal" (personal connection)
- Day 2: Accountability check — "Did you do Day 1?"
- Day 4: Midpoint momentum (or re-engagement if she hasn't logged in)
- Day 7: Finale — finish strong

**After the Challenge (Day 8-20):**
- Day 8: Pure celebration. No selling. "Look at what you just did."
- Day 11-20: A gradual 4-email bridge toward the Mastermind — a testimonial, the full picture, objection answers. Not a hard pitch. A conversation over 2 weeks.

**What makes it different:** She's not getting pitched $2,000 the day after a $47 challenge. The bridge takes 2 weeks. By the time she sees the Mastermind offer, she understands what it is, who it's helped, and how the payment plan works.

**Total emails:** 9 over 20 days

### 3. Madame Estrogen Mastermind ($1,997)

**The moment she buys:**
She just made a $2,000 decision. The confirmation page feels premium — not a generic "thanks for your order." She sees:
- Exactly how to access her course
- A link to join the Empowered Sister Community
- Clear instructions to join the weekly accountability call inside her member area
- A visual timeline of the next 12 weeks
- A mention of her HWN bonus (explained later — right now, focus on Week 1)

**Onboarding (Days 1-4):**
- Day 1: Welcome email — warm, personal, "your one job today is to get logged in and watch Week 1"
- Day 2: How the Mastermind works — the weekly rhythm, the community, the calls
- Day 4: "Have you started Week 1?" — accountability before the first call

**12-Week Journey:**
She doesn't get 12 identical "new module!" emails. The cadence is smarter:
- **Weeks 1-4:** Weekly emails — building the habit, making sure she's engaged
- **Weeks 5-8:** Only a midpoint milestone email at Week 6 — the habit is set, Kajabi handles the drip
- **Weeks 9-12:** Final stretch emails — re-engaging her for the finish line

**Graduation (after Week 12):**
A standalone celebration email. No selling. No HWN pitch. Just: *"12 weeks. You did it."* Plus an invitation to share her story.

**HWN Transition (during the final 15 days of bonus access):**
She gets a soft reintroduction to continuing her HWN access before the 90-day bonus expires, with reminder emails spaced through the final stretch. If she does not convert, bonus access ends without automatic billing.

**Payment plan support:**
If a payment fails, the tone is "let's fix this" — never "your access is at risk" (not until Day 6). We treat a $2,000 customer like a VIP with a billing hiccup.

**Total emails:** 13 content + 3 dunning max = 16 over ~14 weeks

### 4. Hormone Wellness Network ($47/mo or $470/yr)

**The moment she joins:**
The confirmation page should still feel like joining a community, but launch automation should not depend on event dates. The emphasis is on where to start, how to use the membership, and what kind of support exists inside it.

**Onboarding (Days 1-3):**
- Day 1: Welcome — "your one action: post your intro in the community"
- Day 3: The rhythm — how the membership works, what to show up to

**Monthly (ongoing at launch):**
She gets lightweight evergreen value emails through the month:
- Start-of-month orientation
- Mid-month engagement reminder
- End-of-month preview

These should not depend on a live event calendar at launch.

**If she goes quiet:**
We track engagement (event attendance, community posts, logins). If she drops off, she gets a personal check-in: "Haven't seen you lately — everything okay?" If that doesn't work, the team reaches out personally.

**If she cancels:**
Graceful exit. No guilt. For launch, access continues through the billing period and then ends automatically. A cancellation survey can be added later once churn patterns are clearer.

**Renewals:**
Silent. No "you're about to be charged" emails. Those trigger cancellations. Instead, we deliver consistent value all month so she never questions the charge.

**Total emails:** 2 onboarding + ~2-3/month ongoing + win-back if needed
